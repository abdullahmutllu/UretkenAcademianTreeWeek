SUPERNTAURAL

About


Certainly! "Supernatural" is an American television series known for its blend of paranormal, horror, drama, and comedy elements. The series premiered on September 13, 2005, on The WB network and later moved to The CW. Spanning 15 seasons and 327 episodes, it concluded on November 19, 2020.


Created by Eric Kripke, the show initially focuses on two brothers, Sam (Jared Padalecki) and Dean Winchester (Jensen Ackles). The Winchester brothers come from a family of hunters, battling supernatural entities. The story begins as they search for their missing father, leading them to confront various paranormal challenges. Throughout the series, they investigate supernatural occurrences and face demons, angels, ghosts, and other mystical beings.
